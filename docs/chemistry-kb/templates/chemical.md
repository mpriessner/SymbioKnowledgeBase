# Pd(PPh3)4

> One-liner: Palladium(0) catalyst widely used in our lab for Suzuki, Heck, and Stille cross-coupling reactions.

## Practical Usage Notes

### Storage
- Store at 2-8 °C under argon atmosphere in amber glass
- Shelf life: 6 months unopened, 3 months after opening
- Discard if color darkens to brown or black (indicates decomposition)

### Handling
- Air-sensitive: weigh quickly under nitrogen counterflow or in glovebox
- Moisture-sensitive: use anhydrous solvents and dry glassware
- Generates triphenylphosphine oxide as byproduct (may complicate purification)

### Institutional Knowledge
- **Preferred Vendor**: Sigma-Aldrich (cat. 216666) — consistently higher purity than TCI
- **Quality Check**: Should be bright yellow; any grey or brown discoloration means partial decomposition. Run a test reaction with known substrate before committing expensive starting materials
- **Common Issues**: Old batches give lower conversion. Always check lot date before use
- **Typical Loading**: 1-5 mol% depending on substrate reactivity; start with 3 mol% for new substrates
