EXP-CUP-002: Cup Manipulation — Experiment 2
A structured cup manipulation task on a 3×3 grid. Variant 2 with different starting positions and step sequence. Participant follows 15 sequential steps involving cup placement, swaps, stacking, object tracking, and die placement. Instructions use mixed reference types: label, color, and grid position. Two ninja figures (blue and red) are placed inside cups and must be mentally tracked.

Cup Inventory
* Cup A8 — Green
* Cup 8F — Red
* Cup G3 — Yellow
* Cup 5K — Blue
* Cup 2N — Black

Additional Objects
* 1 blue ninja figure (hidden object #1)
* 1 red ninja figure (hidden object #2)
* 1 red die

Starting Position
* Cup 8F (red) → A2
* Cup 5K (blue) → A3
* Cup 2N (black) → B1
* Cup G3 (yellow) → C1
* Cup A8 (green) → C2

Procedures
1. Place the blue ninja inside cup 2N at position B1. Don't look inside any cup from now on.
2. Place the red ninja inside cup 5K at position A3.
3. Move cup 5K from position A3 to position B3.
4. Swap the red cup with the yellow cup. Find both on the grid and switch their positions.
5. Move the cup at position C2 to position A1.
6. Stack cup A8 on top of cup 2N. Find cup A8 on the grid, then place it upside-down on top of 2N.
7. Place the red die on the blue cup. Find the blue cup first.
8. Move the entire stack at position B1 to position C3. Pick up both cups together.
9. Swap the red cup with the yellow cup. Find both on the grid and switch their positions.
10. Remove the top cup from the stack at C3 and place it at position A3.
11. Swap the black cup with cup 8F. Find both on the grid and switch their positions.
12. Move the cup at position B3 to position B1.
13. Swap cup G3 with cup A8. Find both on the grid and switch their positions.
14. Swap the blue cup with the green cup. Find both on the grid and switch their positions.
15. Move the cup at position A3 to position B2.

Recall Questions
* "Point to where you think the blue ninja is."
* "Point to where you think the red ninja is."

Materials
* 5 cups with alphanumeric labels and color bands (A8/green, 8F/red, G3/yellow, 5K/blue, 2N/black)
* 1 blue ninja figure
* 1 red ninja figure
* 1 red die
* 3×3 grid mat (Rows: A/B/C, Columns: 1/2/3)

Reference Types Used
* Steps 1, 2, 3: Label + Position
* Steps 4, 9, 14: Color ↔ Color
* Steps 5, 8, 10, 12, 15: Position → Position
* Steps 6, 13: Label ↔ Label
* Step 7: Color (die placement)
* Step 11: Color ↔ Label (mixed)

Best Practices
* Read one step at a time and wait for confirmation before proceeding
* Never reveal ninja locations during the experiment
* Never reorder or skip steps
* When a step uses color, do NOT also mention the label (and vice versa) — the mixing is intentional
* If participant asks about a cup's location, answer from internal grid state
* Allow participant to say "repeat" or ask "what step am I on?"

Common Pitfalls
* Participants may confuse alphanumeric labels (A8, G3) with grid positions (A1, A3) — this is by design
* Color-referenced steps (4, 7, 9, 11, 14) require visual scanning
* Label-referenced steps (6, 13) require recalling label-to-position mapping
* Tracking two ninjas simultaneously is significantly harder than one
* Stacking (step 6), stack-moving (step 8), and unstacking (step 10) are error-prone
* Participants sometimes try to look inside cups after steps 1–2

Answer Key (experimenter only)
* Blue ninja: position A2, inside cup 2N (black)
* Red ninja: position C1, inside cup 5K (blue)

Ninja Paths
* Blue: B1 (steps 1–7) → C3 (step 8, moved with stack) → A2 (step 11, swapped black↔8F)
* Red: A3 (step 2) → B3 (step 3, moved with 5K) → B1 (step 12, moved with 5K) → C1 (step 14, swapped blue↔green)

Grid: Rows A(top) B(mid) C(bottom), Columns 1(left) 2(center) 3(right).
